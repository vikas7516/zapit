About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's a screening tool that can indicate whether a person has a healthy body weight.

BMI Categories

Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal Weight
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obese

Important Note

BMI is a screening tool and not a diagnostic measure. It doesn't directly measure body fat and may not be accurate for athletes, elderly, or pregnant women. Consult healthcare professionals for comprehensive health assessment.
