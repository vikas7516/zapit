Pro Tips

  • Natural sort handles values like Item 2 and Item 10 in human order. Disable for strict ASCII ordering.
  • Use the random sample field to pull a subset after sorting or shuffling. A sample of 5 returns the first five entries of the current result.
  • Duplicates are compared after trimming and case normalization (unless you enable case sensitivity).
  • Combine sorting and shuffling: sort, then shuffle, or vice versa. The operation badge shows the latest action.
