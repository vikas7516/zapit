Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

Overview

Zapit provides free online tools that run directly in your web browser. Most tools perform processing entirely on your device (client-side). We do not require accounts or registration.

What Data We Process

  • Input data you provide to a tool (e.g., text, images, PDFs) is processed locally in your browser where possible and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Usage data such as anonymized analytics may be collected to improve the site (e.g., page views, feature usage). This never includes your tool inputs or files.
  • Cookies/local storage may be used to remember preferences (e.g., theme, recent tools). These do not contain personal data.

File Handling

For tools that handle files, operations like compression, conversion, or editing happen in your browser whenever technically possible. Files are not permanently stored by Zapit. If a feature requires temporary processing, we will clearly indicate this and limit retention to what’s necessary to provide the functionality.

Third-Party Services

We may use privacy-friendly analytics or content delivery networks (CDNs) for performance. These services receive only minimal technical data required to operate the site and cannot access your tool inputs.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser’s cache/storage to remove local data.
  • You can opt out of non-essential cookies via your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our tools are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
